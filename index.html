About EUROBRIDGE

Let's take a look at Georgia

Eurobridge is a student-based non-governmental organization that aims to overcome social and cultural barriers, promote European values ​​and democratic processes. The organization implements initiatives that promote the active involvement of youth, women, and various socially vulnerable groups in public life.

Eurobridge's activities include the implementation of projects at the local and international levels, which are based on the principles of European integration, human rights protection, education promotion and support for community engagement. The organization actively cooperates with Georgian state agencies, international organizations and other representatives of the non-governmental sector.

Eurobridge is also an active participant in organizing cultural and educational initiatives, which contribute to the promotion of topics of European importance in Georgia and the development of civil society.
